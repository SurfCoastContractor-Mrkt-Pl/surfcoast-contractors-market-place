# SurfCoast Terminology Guide

## Overview
This document defines the official terminology used throughout the SurfCoast platform. Use these terms consistently across all pages, buttons, labels, and messaging.

## Core User Types

### Service Side
| Official Term | Use When | Examples | ❌ Avoid |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Contractor** | Primary term for service providers | "Contractor Account", "Browse Contractors", "Contractor Dashboard" | Entrepreneur, Worker, Service Provider |
| **Client** | Person/business posting work | "Find Clients", "Client Inquiry", "Client Review" | Customer (service side), User |

### Market Side
| Official Term | Use When | Examples | ❌ Avoid |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Vendor** | Person selling at markets | "Vendor Dashboard", "Vendor Booth", "Vendor Review" | Seller, Shop Owner |
| **Consumer** | Person shopping at markets | "Consumer Account", "Consumer Hub" | Customer (market side), Buyer, Shopper |

### General
| Official Term | Use When | Examples | ❌ Avoid |
|---|---|---|---|
| **User** | Generic reference to any registered person | "User authentication", "User data" | Member, Account holder |
| **Platform** | Referring to SurfCoast overall | "Platform fees", "Platform rules" | Website, App, Site |

## Work/Jobs Terminology

| Official Term | Use When | Examples | ❌ Avoid |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Job** | Work posted by clients | "Post a Job", "Browse Jobs", "Job details" | Project, Work, Task, RFP |
| **Scope of Work** | Formal agreement on what contractor will do | "Approve Scope of Work", "Scope details" | Statement of Work, SOW |

| **Timed Session** | Paid communication window | "Start a timed session", "$1.50 per session" | Chat session, Communication window |

## Best Practices

1. **Use official terms consistently** — don't swap between synonyms
2. **Avoid abbreviations** — spell out terms (not "CMP", not "WaveFO" except in specific contexts)
3. **Capitalize correctly:**
   - SurfCoast (company/platform)
   - WAVE OS (always all caps)
   - WAVE FO (all caps, only for field ops)
   - Market Shop (capital M and S)
4. **Context matters** — use "Client" on service side, not "Customer"
5. **Be specific** — "Job posted by client" not "New job available"
6. **Legal documents** can use "SurfCoast Contractors Marketplace (SurfCoast CMP)" for formal registration
