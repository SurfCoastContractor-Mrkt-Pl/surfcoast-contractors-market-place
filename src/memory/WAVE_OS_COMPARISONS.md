## Where WAVE OS & WAVEshop OS Rank Today

- **WAVE OS (For Contractors):** Highly competitive, especially for solo operators and small crews. Aligns with mobile-first and automation-focused leaders like Coast and Jobber, with strong emphasis on compliance and job documentation. Avoids the "10-Click Problem" that plagues legacy systems.

- **WAVEshop OS (For Market Vendors):** Specialized tool for physical market vendors. Flat-rate, zero-commission model for in-person sales is a strong differentiator. Onboarding is designed to quickly get vendors' online profiles and business information set up from scratch — vendors with no existing profile enter their information to build their presence on the platform.

- **The Marketplace Synergy (Unique Selling Proposition):** WAVE OS (Contractors) and WAVEshop OS (Vendors) are distinct software products offered within a marketplace that generates opportunities. This "referral service" model — where software tools facilitate access to new work or new customers — is the biggest competitive edge over pure FO software solutions.

## The Verdict
**Top 5% for both solo/small contractor crews and independent market vendors.** Tailored, functional OS products that avoid the "Dysfunctional" traps, while leveraging the marketplace to provide a unique value proposition: software that helps users find work and grow their business.

## Key Differentiators Summary

### WAVE OS (Contractors) — $19–$125/month tiered
- Offline-first mobile experience
- Photo-based job documentation (before/after required)
- Identity & license verification at onboarding
- Trade Games for skill engagement
- End-to-end job pipeline: Job → Scope → Payment
- Account compliance enforcement (overdue job locks, after-photo deadlines)

### WAVEshop OS (Market Vendors) — $35/month flat
- Zero commission on in-person sales
- Online profile built from scratch during signup (no existing profile needed)
- Inventory tracking, booth schedule management
- Product listings with photos & pricing
- Customer reviews & ratings
- Analytics dashboard
- Flat fee — no transaction surprises
