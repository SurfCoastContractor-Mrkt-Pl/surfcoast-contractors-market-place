# TABLE 17: WHY RESTRICTIONS EXIST

| Restriction | Applies To | Cost/Fee | Reason |
|-------------|-----------|----------|--------|
| **18% Platform Fee** | Contractor payouts | 18% of job amount | Payment processing (2-3%), infrastructure (5%), support/disputes (8%), fraud prevention (2%) |
| **20% Vendor Fee** | Market vendor sales | 20% of sale | Payment processing (2-3%), infrastructure (5%), support/disputes (8%), platform marketing (4%) |
| **72-Hour Photo Deadline** | Contractor after-photos | Access restriction | Ensures fresh evidence of work, prevents stock photos, proves completion timing |
| **$1.50 Messaging Fee** | Client-contractor chat | Per-hour charge | Prevents spam/scams, funds support staff, ensures serious interactions only |
| **14-Day Approval Window** | Client closeout | Account restriction (limited) | Encourages timely decision-making, reduces escrow hold time |
| **After-Photo Lock** | Contractor missing photos | Account lock | Enforces accountability, prevents future job posting until resolved |
| **5+ Before Photos** | Client job posting | Rejection if missing | Ensures clear scope definition, prevents vague requests |
| **Escrow Hold** | All payments | Time delay (pending approval) | Protects both parties, provides dispute resolution window, prevents fraud |
| **Tier Locks** | All users | Job/sales count gate | Prevents feature overwhelm, aligns features with experience level |
