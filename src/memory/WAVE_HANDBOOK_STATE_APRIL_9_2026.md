## Account Lock System

| Lock Type | Who It Hits | Trigger | How to Lift |
|---|---|---|---|
| 72-Hour Photo Lock | Entrepreneur only | Missing after-photos 72hrs after agreed work date | Upload after-photos |
| Rating Block | Non-compliant party only | Either party fails to rate after job close | Submit the rating |
| Overdue Job Lock | Both entrepreneur AND client | Job passes expected completion date without closeout | Close out the scope |

- All locks are **fully automated** — no manual review process
- Locks lift **instantly** when the required action is completed
- Accounts locked for overdue jobs that reach billing cycle end → card removal + account deletion queue
