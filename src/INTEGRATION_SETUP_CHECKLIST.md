## What YOU (App Owner) Need to Do - One Time Setup

### Step 1: Connect Your HubSpot Account
1. Go to https://app.hubspot.com → Settings → Apps & Integrations → Private Apps
2. Create a new private app with these scopes:
   - `crm.objects.contacts.read`
   - `crm.objects.contacts.write`
   - `crm.objects.deals.read`
   - `crm.objects.deals.write`
3. Copy the **Client ID** and **Client Secret**
4. In Base44, I'll authorize your account with these credentials (shared connector)
5. Done—all WAVE Premium users now have HubSpot sync active

### Step 2: Connect Your Notion Account
1. Go to https://www.notion.so/my-integrations
2. Create a new integration and grant:
   - Read content
   - Update content
   - Create pages in databases
3. Copy the **Client ID** and **Client Secret**
4. In Base44, I'll authorize your account with these credentials (shared connector)
5. Done—all WAVE Premium users can now create Notion project pages
