About SurfCoast Contractor Marketplace

SurfCoast Contractor Marketplace was founded by Hector A. Navarrete, a San Diego native, licensed plumber with over a decade of experience, and owner of SurfCoast Plumbing. His mission: create a space where everyday skilled individuals — freelancers, tradespeople, creatives, and self-starters — can connect with real opportunities, grow their reputation, and build something of their own.

Our Mission

We believe "contractor" is not a job title — it is a mindset. It belongs to anyone willing to put in the work, deliver real value, and stand behind what they do. From young hustlers picking up their first job to seasoned professionals running their own operation, SurfCoast is built for the builders, the learners, and the ones who refuse to sit still.

We don't believe in gatekeeping. You don't need a massive company, years of credentials, or a polished brand to get started. If you have skill, discipline, and the drive to improve, you belong here. This platform reflects the values of accountability, growth, and respect for the work.
